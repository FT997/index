About Trönningenäs

Investment AB Trönningenäs is a privately held limited company. Through its subsidiaries, it acquires shares in both publicly traded and privately held companies, primarily within the real estate, energy and industrials sectors.

Through data-driven analysis and a commitment to continuous improvement, our goal is to generate strong risk-adjusted returns on our capital over time. At the same time, we strive to surround ourselves with intelligent, driven, and loyal employees and partners.
